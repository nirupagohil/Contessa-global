About Us

At Contessa, we are passionate about delivering high-quality apparel that combines comfort and style. With in-house production facilities, we ensure that each product meets the highest quality standards.

Vision & Mission

Our goal is to provide high-quality, comfortable garments for men and women across all age groups, with a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and sustainability. We strive to offer premium products at competitive prices while contributing to a greener environment and fostering trust with our clients.

Knitting

Our advanced machinery from Taiwan, Germany, Italy, and other global leaders enables the production of rib, interlock, and intricate designs. We specialize in knitting cotton combed, carded, and polyester blend fabrics ranging from 60 GSM to 250 GSM, ensuring precision and versatility in every piece.

Dyeing

Our dyeing plant is equipped with advanced German machinery and a reverse osmosis system to recycle water for irrigation, supporting our eco-friendly practices. The facility includes state-of-the-art machines for mercerizing, dyeing, pre-shrinking, and compacting, all with a focus on sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Garmenting

With nearly three decades of experience, Contessa's skilled team excels in precision garmenting using imported sewing machines. Our focus on stitching perfection, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction ensures that every garment meets the highest standards of quality.

Garmenting / Dyeing Capacity

(1) Garmenting Capacity: With advanced machinery including flatlock (10), overlock (15), rib cutter (2), single needle (25), and Kaj-button (2) machines, we maintain a production capacity of 30,000 pieces per month. (2) Dyeing Capacity: Our dyeing facility, with a production capacity of 100 tons per month, offers both semi-dyeing and fast-dyeing options to meet various textile needs.
