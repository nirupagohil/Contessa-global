Our Story

"Sigma Textiles & Garments Pvt. Ltd., located in the vibrant city of Ahmedabad, stands as a beacon of excellence in the garments manufacturing industry, boasting an impressive legacy of 35 years. Our comprehensive production process begins at our state-of-the-art Knitting and Dyeing Unit, where we meticulously curate high-quality fabrics tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clientele. We specialize in crafting a wide range of women's apparel, including stylish leggings, comfortable T-shirts, elegant nightwear, cozy pajamas, and essential innerwear, all designed with the modern woman in mind. Each piece is expertly stitched and prepared at our facility, ensuring that every garment reflects our commitment to quality and craftsmanship. With a robust distribution network, we proudly ship our products across India and Globally, making Sigma Textiles & Garments a trusted name for those seeking fashionable and durable clothing. Experience the perfect blend of tradition and innovation with our garments, where quality truly meets excellence.
